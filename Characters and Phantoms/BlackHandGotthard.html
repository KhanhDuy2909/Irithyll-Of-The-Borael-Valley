Black Hand Gotthard

Gotthard, one of king's Black Hand, who fled the castle.
He rose quickly through the knighthood, no surprise to those who have witnessed his swordsmanship

A hunter who served several kings of Lothric. He held the title of "Black Hand", a title that is only bestowed upon hunters that serve successive kings. To date, only three have obtained this title (Black Hand Kamui is one and the other is unknown).
